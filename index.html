About Me

Hello, I'm Ayansh Paliwal

I'm a dedicated student with a passion for technology, programming, and creative problem-solving. Based in India, I'm constantly exploring new technologies and working on innovative projects that challenge me to grow as a developer.


My journey includes mastering all 10 levels of abacus by age 11, programming in multiple languages since age 10, and building robots for competitions. I'm also passionate about sports, holding a Red belt in Karate and winning awards in swimming.


I believe in the power of continuous learning and enjoy sharing my knowledge with others. My goal is to use technology to create meaningful solutions that can make a positive impact on the world, especially in areas like education and sustainability.

Programming

I started coding at age 10, diving into Python, and JavaScript. Since then, I have expanded my knowlange to learning kotlin and dart for app development, C++ for game development.
